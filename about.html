ABOUT


    The "Studdy Buddy" Website is an Informative and Interactive Website that provides information that focuses on the improvement of the students in their productivity in studying. This website contains reliable and verified information gathered by the developers from different sources. This website also includes pictures, and fun and interactive activities that will help users to improve in their study.
The "Study Buddy" website was developed as part of the Study conducted by the grade 12 MAWD students from STI College Kalibo which is entitled, "The Acceptability of having the Study Buddy as an Informative and Interactive Website for the IT Students/Teachers of STI College Kalibo."



PURPOSE & GOAL


    The purpose of the "Study Buddy" website is to help the users study more effectively by providing reliable content about topics such as coping with distractions in studying, tips on reviewing for upcoming exam, different studying techniques, etc.
Its purpose is to determine the Acceptability and Effectiveness of using a website in providing information.
